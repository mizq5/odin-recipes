Easy Cake

Recipe and Ingredients

Quick and easy cake.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of cake mix
  • 2 eggs
  • oil
  • water

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Add all ingredients into bowl.
  3. Pour into greased cake pan.
  4. Bake for time listed on box.
  5. Cook until done toothpick comes out clean.
